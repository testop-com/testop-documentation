## Interview Objectives

- Understand daily system usage patterns
- Identify pain points and inefficiencies
- Gather feature requests and improvement ideas
- Assess user satisfaction and training needs
- Document workarounds and manual processes

**Estimated Duration:** 30-45 minutes

---

## Pre-Interview Checklist

- [ ] Meeting scheduled and confirmed
- [ ] Interview location/video link confirmed
- [ ] Recording permission obtained (if applicable)
- [ ] Note-taking tools ready
- [ ] Background research on interviewee's role completed
- [ ] Previous interview notes reviewed (if follow-up)

## Introduction (2-3 minutes)

**Script:**
_"Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today. I'm [Your Name], and I'm conducting an assessment of our internal system to understand how it's working for everyone and identify opportunities for improvement._

_This conversation will take about 30-45 minutes. There are no right or wrong answers - I'm genuinely interested in your honest experience and opinions. Everything you share will be documented and used to make recommendations for system improvements._

_[If recording:] Is it okay if I record this conversation for note-taking purposes? The recording will only be used internally and will be kept confidential._

_Do you have any questions before we begin?"_
